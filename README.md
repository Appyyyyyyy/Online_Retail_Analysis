## 📊 Online Retail Analysis Dashboard (2011)

### 🔍 Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing online retail data from 2011. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making in revenue growth, customer targeting, and market expansion.

---

### 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze revenue trends over time  
- Identify top-performing countries and customers  
- Understand global demand distribution  
- Derive actionable business insights  

---

### 📊 Dashboard Components

#### 1. Monthly Revenue (2011)
- Visualizes monthly revenue trends  
- Highlights seasonality and peak sales periods  

#### 2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue
- Identifies highest revenue-generating countries  
- Shows geographic concentration of sales  

#### 3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
- Highlights high-value customers  
- Supports customer retention strategies  

#### 4. Demand by Country
- Displays distribution of order demand across countries  
- Enables comparison of demand patterns globally  

---

### 📈 Key Insights
- Revenue exhibits strong seasonal patterns with clear peak periods  
- A small number of countries contribute the majority of total revenue  
- High-value customers account for a significant portion of overall sales  
- Demand varies significantly across regions, indicating different market dynamics  

---

### 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing countries  
- Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers  
- Optimize inventory planning based on seasonal trends  
- Explore expansion opportunities in underperforming regions
