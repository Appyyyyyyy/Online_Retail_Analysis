📊 Online Retail Analysis Dashboard (2011)
🔍 Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing online retail data from 2011. The goal is to extract actionable business insights related to revenue trends, geographic performance, and customer contribution.
The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making for business growth and customer targeting.
📊 Dashboard Features
The analysis focuses on four key areas:
1. Monthly Revenue (2011)
Tracks revenue trends across months
Identifies seasonality and peak sales periods
2. Top 10 Countries by Revenue
Highlights countries contributing the most to total revenue
Shows geographic concentration of business
3. Top 10 Customers by Revenue
Identifies high-value customers
Useful for retention and targeted marketing strategies
4. Demand by Country
Visualizes order demand across different regions
Compares demand distribution globally
📈 Key Insights
Revenue shows clear seasonal spikes, indicating strong peak periods
A small number of countries dominate overall revenue contribution
High-value customers account for a significant portion of total sales
Demand distribution varies widely across countries, suggesting market-specific strategies
💡 Business Recommendations
Focus marketing and expansion strategies on top-performing countries
Implement loyalty programs for high-value customers
Optimize inventory planning based on seasonal demand trends
Explore growth opportunities in underperforming regions
🛠️ Tools Used
Tableau (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)
Microsoft Excel (Data Source)
